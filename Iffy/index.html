If you think you have food poisoning in Santa Clara, please complete this form. The info will then be shared to the Santa Clara Department of Public Health so they can take any necessary action.

Here’s more information about what we do:

  1. Currently Santa Clara Health Department registers complaints only through email and phone call.
  2. We automate this process of connecting health department officals with people who may be facing health issues related to food poisoning.
  3. Also, we make the process of registering such complaints much easier by providing a simple and concise form.

We used a supervised machine learning algorithm to search Twitter for tweets related to food poisoning. We try to present the closest and most plausible tweets to reduce the human effort needed to find those people. Then iffy replies to the tweets with a link back to this page.

What happened?

Please tell us what food items you consumed, what time of the day, what was your first symptom, and for how long you were sick.
